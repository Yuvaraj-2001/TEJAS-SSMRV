Report on National level E-Quiz competition quizvid-19

Youth red cross club of SSMRV had organized a national level E-quiz competition for 3days i.e on 28-08-2020, 29-08-2020 and 30-08-2020, through google platform(google froms) there were nearly 200+ students from all over Karnataka and other states took part in this 3days E-quiz competition.

Dr. Prathima vijay co-ordinator of SSMRV YRC forwarded the link to Dr. Anil kumar principal of SSMRV college, faculty members, and for other college professors and Asst professors on day before the start of quiz to circulate the link of google form i.e on 27-08-2020, On day one of the competition there were more than 90+ students, on day two there were nearly 75 students, on day three there were nearly 50+ students from 45+ various institutions from all over Karnataka and from other states actively participated and made this event a grand success.
